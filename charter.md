## 1. PURPOSE & BUSINESS CASE
Build a data-grounded multi-agent analysis system that produces draft rankings and a "My Guys" list that measurably beat market consensus (ADP/ECR) for Nick's league, and that compounds season over season via archived data and postmortems.
Why this matters to me: competitive edge in a 12-team league + hands-on AI system-building and RCA skill development.

## 2. OBJECTIVES (SMART)
- **O1:** By Aug 27, 2026 (board freeze), deliver a frozen draft board (position ranks, tiers, overall board) generated by the validated pipeline.
- **O2:** By Aug 27, 2026, deliver a My Guys list (5–10 players) with pre-registered theses and invalidation triggers.
- **O3:** By Aug 19, 2026 (M5), the pipeline beats ADP and ECR baselines on BOTH frozen-world backtests (per §3 metrics).
- **O4:** By Jan 31, 2027, complete the postmortem scoring all of the above and produce next-season adjustments.

## 3. SUCCESS METRICS (formulas in Appendix M of phase0-charter-design.md — binding)
- **PRIMARY — SM1:** My Guys hit rate ≥ 60% (dual condition: beats ADP-implied positional finish AND returns startable value)
- **SECONDARY — SM2:** Final board Spearman correlation vs. actual end-of-season value EXCEEDS both ADP's and ECR's, overall and in ≥ 3 of 4 positions
- **TERTIARY — SM3:** Tier accuracy ≥ 70% (players finishing within assigned tier ±1; starters weighted 2×)
- **PROCESS — SM4:** Backtest gate (O3) passed before freeze; daily capture ≥ 90% uptime Jul 24 → draft; zero uncited stats in final deliverables

**Player universes (fixed, anti-cherry-picking):** QB24 / RB48 / WR60 / TE24 by consensus at freeze.
**Games threshold for PPG scoring:** minimum 8 games played.

## 4. SCOPE
**IN SCOPE (v1):** season-long pre-draft rankings for QB, RB, WR, TE; this league's settings only; daily silent data capture + weekly Thursday analysis brief (daily ramp from Aug 19); post-season scoring and postmortem.
**OUT OF SCOPE (v1) — requires a charter change:** weekly lineup/start-sit advice; trade valuation; DFS/betting; other leagues; kickers, DST, IDP rankings (K/DST are drafted in the final two rounds per board annotation — deliberate, not an oversight); public/productized version.
